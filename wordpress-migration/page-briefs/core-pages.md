### FAQ (15 questions)

**Q: What does a digital marketing consultant do?**  
A digital marketing consultant audits your current marketing, identifies the highest-leverage growth opportunities, and builds the strategy, content, and systems needed to hit measurable growth targets — SEO, paid media, content, and automation included.

**Q: How much does a digital marketing consultant cost?**  
Rewansh's consulting starts at $349 + tax for a one-time strategy engagement, with ongoing growth partnerships from $549 + tax per month. Enterprise engagements are custom-priced based on scope.

**Q: How long does it take to see results from digital marketing?**  
Most clients see initial traction within 30-60 days from paid media and content systems, while SEO and organic growth typically compound over 3-6 months.

**Q: Do you work with small businesses or only enterprises?**  
Both. Rewansh works with startups, D2C brands, and SaaS companies through flexible consulting and growth-partner plans, as well as enterprises that need custom, large-scale marketing infrastructure.

**Q: What industries does Rewansh specialize in?**  
Rewansh primarily works with D2C brands, SaaS founders, fintech companies, and service-based businesses looking to scale customer acquisition through digital channels.

**Q: What's the difference between a digital marketing consultant and an agency?**  
An agency spreads your account across a team, often with a junior day-to-day contact. A consultant like Rewansh works with you directly — no account manager layer — bringing hands-on strategy and execution rather than delegating your work to whoever's available.

**Q: Is hiring a digital marketing consultant worth it?**  
It's worth it when you need senior-level strategy without the overhead of a full-time hire or a retainer-locked agency. Most clients recoup the cost within a quarter through lower customer acquisition costs and a functioning content or paid-media system that keeps running after the engagement.

**Q: Do you offer fractional CMO or outsourced CMO services?**  
Yes — the Growth Partner and Enterprise plans function as a fractional CMO engagement: ongoing strategic ownership of your marketing direction, channel prioritization, and reporting, without the cost of a full-time executive hire.

**Q: What's included in a marketing consultant retainer?**  
The Growth Partner retainer includes full SEO, paid media, and content operations, AI-powered content distribution, a dedicated consultant, and bi-weekly performance sprints — see the pricing section for exact deliverables at each tier.

**Q: Do you work with venture-backed or Series A/B startups?**  
Yes. Funded startups typically need to show growth efficiency to investors — Rewansh builds the SEO, paid, and content infrastructure to hit those targets without over-spending on acquisition ahead of the next raise.

**Q: How do I choose the right digital marketing consultant?**  
Look for direct access to the person doing the work (not a sales rep), a clear audit-first process before any spend commitment, transparent pricing, and evidence of hands-on channel expertise rather than generic "full-service" claims.

**Q: Can a consultant replace an in-house marketing hire?**  
For most startups and small teams, yes — a consultant covers strategy and execution at a fraction of a full-time marketing hire's salary and benefits, and can scale down once systems are running. Larger teams often use a consultant to lead strategy while an in-house coordinator handles day-to-day execution.

**Q: Do you work with clients in India as well as internationally?**  
Yes. Rewansh works with founders and marketing teams across India, the United States, United Kingdom, UAE, Germany, and Canada, Australia, and Singapore — every engagement is fully remote, with flexible scheduling to cover overlapping working hours across time zones. See the full country breakdown.

**Q: Do you work with businesses in Indore and Hyderabad?**  
Yes. Rewansh works with founders and marketing teams in Indore, Hyderabad, Mumbai, Bangalore, Delhi NCR, and Pune as a fully remote consultant — no local office is required, since strategy calls, reporting, and campaign execution all happen over video and async communication.

**Q: Do you help with martech stack audits and tool consolidation?**  
Yes. Many clients arrive with five to ten disconnected tools — CRM, email platform, analytics, ad platforms — that don't talk to each other. Rewansh audits the existing martech stack, cuts redundant or unused tools, and connects what's left into a single reporting and automation system instead of a pile of point solutions.
