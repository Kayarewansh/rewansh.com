FAQ

Questions I get all the time.

Still have questions? Book a free strategy call and I'll answer everything.

Do you work with clients outside the US?
Yes — while I specialize in the US market, I work with clients globally. My systems and strategies are built for English-speaking markets primarily, but I've worked with brands across the UK, Canada, and Australia as well.
How long before I see results?
Most clients see meaningful traction within 30–60 days. Content systems and automation start producing leads within weeks. Brand authority and organic growth compounds over 3–6 months. I'll set realistic expectations on our first call.
What makes you different from other agencies?
I work with a small number of clients at a time — which means you get direct access to me, not a junior account manager. I'm an operator, not just a consultant. I've built and run campaigns myself, and I use AI tools that most agencies haven't even heard of yet.
Do you require long-term contracts?
The Growth and Done-For-You plans have a 3-month minimum — because meaningful results take time and I won't take on clients I can't genuinely help. After that, it's month-to-month. The Strategy Session is a one-time engagement.
What industries do you specialize in?
I primarily work with D2C brands, SaaS founders, course creators, coaches, and personal brand builders. If you're selling a product or service online and you want more visibility and conversions — I can help.
