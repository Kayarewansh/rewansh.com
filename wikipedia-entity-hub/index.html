History

Background.

Rewansh was founded in 2025 by Rewansh Kayare as an independent digital marketing consultancy, built around direct, founder-led client engagement rather than a traditional agency account-management structure. The practice expanded from core marketing services — SEO, paid media, and content strategy — into IT infrastructure and enterprise IT management, reflecting client demand for a single consultant who can own both the marketing strategy and the technical systems it depends on.
